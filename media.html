I worked with three others to create a Twitter bot that can like, retweet, and tweet tweets related to Georgia Tech. It can also reply to other Twitter users that mention the bot. The biggest issue our group had was being unable to gain elevated access for the Twitter developer account, so we were unable to test our code, but we eventually got it!
I worked with three others to create a cookie clicker game. I mostly contributed to the art and ideation, although many of my ideas ended up not being used. For example, we wanted to make the game slightly more difficult and not an exact cookie clicker clone, so I suggested having the cookies appear in random positions on a grid, making it resemble a sort of aim trainer game. However, the head coder decided it may be too ambitious for the deadline and it was scrapped, though we may decide to implement it if we ever go back to it. We also had ideas for an alien theme, where the player has to bake cookies to appease the aliens, but that idea, along with all my artwork for it, was also scrapped.
